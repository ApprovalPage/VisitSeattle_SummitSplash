The Countdown to Summit is On

In just a few months, the Emerald City will unveil a new crown jewel. Starting in January 2023, the Seattle Convention Center’s Summit building — the first high-rise convention center in North America — will open its doors to transform the city’s convention package.

With more than 570,000 square feet of additional event space, the new building pairs with the existing Arch building — just one-and-a-half blocks away — to double the capacity of the entire campus.

Learn how you can take your meetings to new heights at visitseattle.org/meetings.
