Experience a Heightened Sense of Wow

The Emerald City is about to unveil a new crown jewel. Starting in January 2023, the Seattle Convention Center’s Summit building — the first high-rise convention center in North America — will open its doors to transform the city’s convention package.

With more than 570,000 square feet of additional event space, the new building pairs with the existing Arch building — just one-and-a-half blocks away — to double the capacity of the entire campus. And with thoughtfully designed viewpoints, Summit offers attendees multiple ways to soak in Seattle’s surroundings without even leaving the building.

Learn how you can take your meetings to new heights at visitseattle.org/meetings.
