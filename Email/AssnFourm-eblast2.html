The Countdown to Summit is On

When Seattle Convention Center opens the Summit building in January, it will transform the city’s convention package.

The first high-rise convention center in North America offers more than 570,000 square feet of additional event space, and pairs with the existing Arch building — just one-and-a-half blocks away — to double the capacity of the entire campus.

Moreover, Summit offers multiple ways to soak in Seattle’s surroundings without even leaving the building. Summit’s stunning 58,000-square-foot ballroom at the top offers panoramic city views. And the building’s iconic suspended 144-stair ‘Hillclimb’, designed with built-in seating, offers west-facing views of Pike Place Market and Puget Sound just down the street.

Learn how you can take your meetings to new heights at visitseattle.org/meetings.
